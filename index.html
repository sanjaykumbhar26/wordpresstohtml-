PATRICK STORCH MORTGAGE TEAM

  • Loan processing systems are all done in house allowing us to be able to close the loan quicker.
  • We utilize local appraisers that know the area.
  • We, as a direct lender, do not always keep the loan on our books. We have the ability to shop your mortgage among aggregators allowing us much more flexibility in programs and pricing.
  • We have step by step communication pieces in place to notify you throughout the process.
  • Because we only offer home loans, it enables us to spend more time on our clients and excel at what we do best.

BIG BANKS

  • Loan processing is generally not processed at the actual branch but at the bank’s processing center which could be located out of your area.
  • Banks generally use appraisal management companies with no local knowledge of the area.
  • Banks set their own notes based on the lending requirements without any flexibility.
  • Banks do not have the same automated communication system, leaving you dependent on their originator to keep you updated on the progress of the loan.
    Waiting on the loan originator to take time out to contact you may cause delays.
