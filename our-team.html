Our Team

The Mortgage Firm Tampa provides a team culture dedicated to creating and enriching powerful relationships with both clients and business partners. Their foundations are integrity, communication, and an aim to strengthen the Tampa Bay area through community involvement.

The Mortgage Firm Tampa is led by Patrick Storch, Loan Originator and Producing Branch Manager, with over 27 years of experience in the mortgage industry. He prides himself in being client oriented and in honor of his achievements, has earned the Circle of Champions award for Top Producer at the Mortgage Firm Tampa. Storch has also been awarded Top 1% Mortgage Originators in America.

Storch has built a team that has been known to be so resilient and hardworking that they have become a model for other branches. They are consistently ranked as one of the top branches among the Mortgage Firm Corporation.

The Mortgage Firm Tampa is run with the “work hard, play hard” mentality. Storch and his team work relentlessly throughout the week to put together loans and educate themselves. As a result, they are rewarded with monthly team outings and bonding activities.

Storch also takes pleasure in sending his team to seminars nationwide to educate them on product knowledge and team structure, which creates exemplary services for clients and referral partners. His legacy is to train young individuals who want to venture into the mortgage business and to promote them within his team.
